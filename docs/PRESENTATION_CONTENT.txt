SLIDE 2: PROBLEM STATEMENT
================================================================================
Title: The Challenge in Sports Analytics

Problem Statement:
Predicting NBA game outcomes is inherently complex and challenging due to:

• Multiple Variables: Team performance depends on numerous factors including player statistics, team chemistry, home/away advantage, and recent form

• Data Overload: Vast amounts of historical game data exist, but manual analysis is time-consuming and prone to human bias

• Inconsistent Predictions: Traditional methods and gut feelings often lead to inaccurate forecasts

• Lack of Accessible Tools: Most prediction systems are proprietary, expensive, or require advanced technical knowledge

Key Question: How can we leverage historical NBA data to create accurate, accessible, and automated game outcome predictions?
